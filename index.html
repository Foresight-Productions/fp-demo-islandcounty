Our Platform

What Island County Republicans
Are Fighting For

Washington State's progressive leadership threatens the values that define Island County. Here's where we draw the line.

Second Amendment

We will never allow Olympia to strip Island County residents of their constitutional right to keep and bear arms. Not now. Not ever.

Military & Veterans

NAS Whidbey Island is the backbone of our community. We fight for full military funding, veterans' benefits, and respect for those who serve.

Lower Taxes

Washington has no income tax — and we intend to keep it that way. We oppose every new tax, fee, and assessment coming out of Olympia.

Parental Rights

Parents — not school boards, not state agencies — have the final word on their children's education, values, and healthcare decisions.

Public Safety

We back law enforcement unconditionally. Safe streets in Oak Harbor, Coupeville, and Langley require accountability and strong community policing.

Election Integrity

Every legal vote must be counted and every illegal vote must be prevented. We fight for transparent, secure, and trustworthy elections in Island County.

Who We Are

The Conservative Heart of Island County, Washington

Established under Washington State Code (RCW 29A.80.030), we are the official Republican Party of Island County — serving Oak Harbor, Coupeville, Langley, and every precinct across Whidbey and Camano Islands. We are a community of veterans, fishermen, farmers, business owners, and families who believe in American exceptionalism and the power of principled conservative leadership.
