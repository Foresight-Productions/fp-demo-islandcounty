What We Do

Advancing Conservative
Values Across Island County

From endorsing proven candidates to registering new voters, we put boots on the ground so Republican values win at every level of government.

01

Candidate Endorsements

We rigorously vet and endorse conservative candidates for local, state, and federal offices — so Island County voters know exactly who stands for their values on election day.

02

Voter Registration Drives

Our outreach teams work neighborhoods, community events, and local organizations to make sure every conservative in Island County is registered and ready to vote.

03

PCO Recruitment & Organization

Precinct Committee Officers are the backbone of local Republican politics. We recruit, train, and support PCOs across every precinct to keep conservative leadership strong from the ground up.

04

Community Forums & Events

We host candidate debates, civic forums, and community events throughout the year that keep Island County residents engaged, informed, and connected to the issues that matter most to them.

05

Civic Education & Outreach

From constitutional education workshops to ballot initiative training, we arm Island County Republicans with the knowledge and tools to be effective citizens and advocates in their communities.

About Us

The Official Republican Party of Island County

Established under Washington State Code (RCW 29A.80.030), the Island County Republican Party is the official home for conservative voters in Oak Harbor, Coupeville, Langley, and every corner of our island community. We believe in the power of grassroots organizing and the strength of principled leadership.
